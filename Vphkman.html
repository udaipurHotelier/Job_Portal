Housekeeping Manager

Castle In Hotel

Udaipur Full Time 14000 - 18000

Job description

A housekeeping guest service agent (GSA) at a hotel is responsible for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in guest rooms and public areas, and providing a friendly and professional service to guests

Responsibility

  • Clean and maintain guest rooms, public areas, and other designated spaces following established procedures and standards. This includes making beds, vacuuming, dusting, and replenishing amenities.
  • Replenish linens, towels, and guest supplies in rooms. Maintain an inventory of cleaning supplies and report when supplies are running low.
  • Perform thorough inspections of guest rooms to ensure they meet quality and cleanliness standards. Report any maintenance or repair issues to the appropriate department.
  • Provide a friendly and professional demeanor when interacting with guests, addressing their requests, and ensuring their comfort and satisfaction
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization in housekeeping carts, storage areas, and equipment.
  • Document room status and work performed in hotel management systems. Report lost and found items to the supervisor
  • Respect guests' privacy and ensure the security of their belongings during cleaning and room servicing.
